Welcome to my little website in this little corner of the net.

My intentions for this site are simply to document some of my university studies milestones, and some of my volunteering too. It is primarily a little plaything for me to tinker with as a hobby, it’s not intended to be a slick portfolio site which sort of goes without saying. I like the lo-fi aesthetic of late 90’s personal homepages. I enjoy cooking so I’ve added some recipe posts too. Above all else, I enjoy typing to myself.

This site has undergone many different looks as I keep tinkering with new designs. As a result post dates are not always exactly correct as several have had to be reposted after clean installs. However, they are all in correct chronological order.

  • Design aesthetic: Pared-back minimalist 1996 Geocities homepage
