Website for St Mary the Virgin Church

’ve been working on another website for a little while. This one is for St Mary the Virgin Church in Arbroath, they didn’t have one so asked me if I’d do one.

I spoke with the Reverend of the church and the brief he gave was to have a site that was different to all the other sites nowadays. He didn’t want boring and conventional, he wanted fun and different, interesting, and interactive. It was settled upon to have a layout of boxes that represent links, and which animate on mouse-over. He also didn’t want a conventional banner and navigation bar. In the end I implemented a hamburger menu bar discreetly in the corner, this was because some pages went 2 layers deep with the possibility of further. I thought this necessitated a menu so users could find their way back home.

The site went live earlier this week. I have mixed feelings about it as I like conventional layouts with all the standard elements. However, this is a bit wacky, which may have been one of the keywords in the brief.
